Watch "Ninja Tea"

 

  "Ninja Tea" was created as a class project at the Savannah College of Art and Design. 2D Collaborative Project is a class which allows animation graduate students an opportunity to develop a film. Our group of four animators created "Ninja Tea" in ten weeks. During the course, we pitched ideas to each other, developed the chosen concept, and then produced the entire short.
  This film was animated in Flash using Cintiqs.
